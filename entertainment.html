THE WEBs


The internet is the main reason you are able to know how much I love Poland by reading whatever you call this website, but it is not what this section is all about. The following are some of the most fascinating things I found in the internet, the most played songs in my playlist, and most watched YouTube channels.

SONGS

Polish songs are a great source to learn the language, not to mention that they sound really good too. Below are my most favorite Polish songs, some of them I have known because of my Polish friends, one of them requesting (not entirely) a cover of Chryzatemy Złociste.


Ach nie mnie jednej - The Dumplings

First off, we got ourselves The Dumplings. I couldn't really focus on learning the language here because I enjoy the beat more than what I should be learning. It is a pop song produced under their album nowOsiecka in 2017.









Ściernisko - Golec Uorkiestra

This is a really good song to hear for the first time especially if it does not come with corona. Golec uOrkiestra is a folk-rock band, which is a combination of two of my favorite music genres, no wonder whhy I like this song. Unfortunately, people attach this kind of cheerful songs to memes, so it also became popular due to memes among other ways.











Oczy Szeroko by Łzy

When you listen to radio stations of foreign languages for the first time, all you want to hear are the DJs talking alot to learn conversational phrases, but after a lot of painful attempts to understand what they are talking about, you may want to wait for the songs to come up soon. Oczy Szeroko by Łzy is the first one I heard when I tuned into one of the best Polish FM stations, it's some sort of an emo song because it is about hearbreak, and it is beautiful.








Gdzie jest biały węgorz? by Cypis

Unfortunately, I couldn't find the Facebook meme from which I knew about this song. I would just like to tell you that some songs are more enjoyable to sing, cover, and send to someone before reading its translation in your native language. Gdzie jest biały węgorz is absolutely one of those songs. You have to trust me on this one.









Lights by Ralph Kamiński

Although the lyrics are in English, because of Ralph Kamiński I have known this song. I like his songs, but this one I can finally understand fully. It is kind of a break for me in that respect.












VIDEOS

DISCLAIMER: I do not promote anything here, I just want to show these to you because they really are either a great entertainment or a really interesting thing, okay?

I Love Poland by Hazel

If you are surprised that a song like this exists, I am too. It is actually a great song about Poland, featuring some beautiful streets in the country, plus they say they love Poland, and they teach you one of the best words to learn to understand Poles 50% of the time - "kurwa" (Artur from YouTube, 2020). Here's the link if you want to learn how to use this word properly, because I will not feature that very informative video here.












Stand-Up Comedies

Stand-up comedians like Christopher Matthew and Piotrek Szumowski are great ones. I really love Poland that even if after a whole day I get tired and still want to know alot about the culture, I watch these comedians because they also tell stories in a fun way, making me remember the details more (although most of them are maybe just made up, but still there are some truth in them). This one (called Babcia) is good, but there are lots like this in their channels.
